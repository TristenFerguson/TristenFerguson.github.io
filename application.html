Overview

Cookie Clicker Pro is an addictive idle-clicking game where you bake cookies by clicking on a giant cookie. As you accumulate cookies, you can purchase upgrades, unlock new features, and automate production. Your goal is to bake as many cookies as possible while upgrading your cookie production efficiency.

How To Play

  1. Click on the giant to bake cookies.
  2. Earn cookies to buy upgrades such as automatic clicking.
  3. Keep clicking and upgrading to unlock factories, grandmas, and time machines!
