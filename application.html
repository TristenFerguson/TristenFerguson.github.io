Welcome to Cookie Clicker Pro!

Cookie Clicker Pro is an addictive incremental game where you click on a cookie to earn points and unlock upgrades.

How to Play

Follow these simple steps to start playing:

  1. Click on the big cookie to earn points.
  2. Use your points to buy upgrades and multipliers.
  3. Keep clicking and upgrading to maximize your score!

Game Interface

Feature Description
Big Cookie The main button to click for earning points.
Upgrade Shop Spend points to buy upgrades that increase efficiency.
Statistics Check your total cookies, clicks per second, and achievements.
