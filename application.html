Cookie Clicker Pro

About the Game

Cookie Clicker Pro is a fun and addictive incremental game where players click on a giant cookie to earn points. As you accumulate cookies, you can purchase upgrades to automate the process and increase your earnings.

How to Play

Follow these simple steps to get started:

  • Click on the giant cookie to earn points.
  • Use the points to buy upgrades, such as auto-clickers.
  • Continue upgrading to maximize your cookie production!
